# Brand Guidelines

## Product Name
- **RotoAgent** (one word, capital R, capital A)
- Tagline: "Transform 60 minutes of daily analysis into a single tap"
- Powered by the ROTOGPT engine

## Hard Rules
1. **NEVER use the word "AI" in any external content.** Not in marketing, tweets, ads, app descriptions, emails. Never. This is a permanent brand rule.
2. No dashes in copy (except before signoffs). Use colons, commas, periods instead.
3. Don't call it a "bot" or "AI agent" externally. It's a tool, an app, a lineup optimizer.

## Voice & Tone
- Casual, knowledgeable fantasy baseball player
- Not corporate. Not salesy. Not hype-y.
- Like texting a buddy who's really good at fantasy baseball
- Confident but not arrogant
- Data-driven but not dry

## Acceptable Terms
✅ "lineup optimizer"
✅ "smart analysis"
✅ "automated lineup management"
✅ "data-driven recommendations"
✅ "your fantasy baseball edge"

## Avoid
❌ "AI-powered"
❌ "artificial intelligence"
❌ "machine learning"
❌ "algorithm" (sounds too techy)
❌ "revolutionary" / "game-changing" (too hype)
