# Strategic Assets

## First-Party User Database

**What we have:** A growing database of verified Yahoo Fantasy Baseball roto league players with email addresses.

**Why it matters:** This list doesn't exist anywhere else. FantasyPros, ESPN, Yahoo, Reddit: none of them offer a segmented, opted-in list of confirmed roto league players with direct contact info. Every user who logs into RotoAgent via Yahoo OAuth gives us:

- ✅ Email address
- ✅ Yahoo GUID (unique identifier)
- ✅ Confirmation they play Yahoo Fantasy Baseball
- ✅ Confirmation they play roto format specifically
- ✅ League data (number of leagues, league settings, team info)
- ✅ Usage patterns (what features they use, when they're active)

**Current size:** 33 users (as of March 23, 2026). Growing daily through organic App Store search alone, with zero marketing spend.

**Key insight:** Users don't leave this database when they cancel a subscription. Even free users who never convert are confirmed roto players we can contact. The database grows with every login regardless of payment status.

## How to Leverage It

### Short Term (Now)
- Email paid subscribers asking for App Store/Play Store reviews
- Email all users announcing new features (free trial, new tools)
- Survey users to learn what they want (product research)

### Medium Term (100+ users)
- Weekly email digest: lineup tips, trending players, league insights
- Re-engagement campaigns for lapsed users ("Your league draft is coming up")
- Referral program ("Invite a leaguemate, both get bonus optimizations")

### Long Term (1,000+ users)
- Segment by engagement level, league size, subscription status
- Seasonal campaigns: pre-draft hype, opening day, trade deadline, playoff push
- Cross-sell future products (NFL fantasy, dynasty leagues, DFS tools)
- Partnership leverage ("We have X thousand verified fantasy baseball players")

### Offseason Value
- Draft prep content starting in January/February
- "Your league is setting up" reminders when Yahoo opens new seasons
- Keep users warm with mock drafts, projections, rankings content

## Rules
- Never sell or share user data externally
- All emails must provide value, not just promotion
- Respect unsubscribes immediately
- This is a trust asset: users gave us their Yahoo login. Don't abuse it.
